About Joel Michael Poirier

Since the time I could talk I was telling stories. Coming up with ways to make the movies I saw more dynamic or the books I read have more depth. I was constantly looking for ways to make things more creative. This led to years of writing short stories and finally getting into film myself. I started working with people who were creatives like myself and fell in love with working as a team to achieve the dreams and goals we wanted. I made a few short films myself and continue to this day to write creatively. This bled into ways to start looking for more creative outlets where I could tell people's stories. I found myself creating a lot of RPG games in my spare time, with an application called RPG Maker MV. I found myself creating plugins for the game, which uses JavaScript, and became very interested in coding. After diving more into it I wanted to build my skills more and surround myself with people who were different than me but all had the same goal. Every day I strive to learn more about my job, about those around me, and create new things.
